<p><ul><li>Strip Cut</li><li>Cross Cut</li><li>Micro Cut</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper products shredder market is categorized into three main types: strip cut, cross cut, and micro cut. Strip cut shredders slice paper into long strips, offering basic security but lower protection for sensitive information. Cross cut shredders provide greater security by cutting paper into smaller pieces, making it harder to reconstruct documents. Micro cut shredders offer the highest level of security, transforming paper into tiny particles, ideal for confidential materials. Each type caters to varying security needs and user preferences.</p></p>
