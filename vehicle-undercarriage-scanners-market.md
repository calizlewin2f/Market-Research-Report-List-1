<p><ul><li>Permanent (Embedded in The Road)</li><li>Fixed (Attached to The Road's Surface)</li><li>Portable (Mobile)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle undercarriage scanners market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Permanent (Embedded in The Road)**: These are integrated into road surfaces for continuous monitoring, providing real-time data on vehicle undercarriages as they pass over.</p><p>2. **Fixed (Attached to The Road's Surface)**: These systems are securely mounted on the road and offer consistent scanning capabilities, yet are not fully embedded, allowing for some mobility.</p><p>3. **Portable (Mobile)**: These scanners are designed for flexible use and can be relocated as needed, making them ideal for temporary checkpoints or inspections. each type serves specific operational needs in enhancing vehicle security and inspection processes.</p></p>
