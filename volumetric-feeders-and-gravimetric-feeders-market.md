<p><p>Volumetric feeders measure and dispense materials based on a fixed volume, ensuring a consistent flow rate regardless of the material's density. They are typically simpler and more cost-effective but may lack precise accuracy. Gravimetric feeders, on the other hand, weigh materials as they are dispensed, providing higher precision and control by adjusting feed rates based on real-time measurements. Both market types cater to various industries, including plastics, food, and pharmaceuticals, where accurate material handling is essential for production efficiency.</p></p>
