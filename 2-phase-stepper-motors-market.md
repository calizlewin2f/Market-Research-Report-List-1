<p><ul><li>Bipolar Type</li><li>Unipolar Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 2-phase stepper motors market is primarily divided into two types: bipolar and unipolar. Bipolar stepper motors utilize two coils and require alternating current to operate, providing higher torque and efficiency but necessitating more complex control systems. Unipolar stepper motors, on the other hand, have two separate windings per phase and allow easier control, making them user-friendly but generally offering lower torque. Both types cater to various applications in robotics, automation, and precision control systems, influencing their market dynamics.</p></p>
