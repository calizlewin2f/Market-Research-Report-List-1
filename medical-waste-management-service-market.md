<p><p>Medical waste management services focus on the safe disposal of healthcare-related waste to prevent environmental contamination and health risks. Incineration involves burning waste at high temperatures, effectively reducing it to ash and gases while sterilizing harmful pathogens. Autoclaving uses steam under pressure to disinfect waste, ensuring it is safe for disposal. Chemical disinfection employs chemical agents to neutralize pathogens in waste before disposal. Each method aims to minimize potential dangers associated with medical waste, ensuring compliance with health and safety regulations.</p></p>
