<p><ul><li>Mechanical Water Pump</li><li>Electric Water Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive water pump market is primarily divided into mechanical and electric water pumps. Mechanical water pumps rely on the engine's belt system to circulate coolant through the engine and cooling system, offering reliability and simplicity. In contrast, electric water pumps operate independently of the engine's speed, allowing for more efficient cooling control and reduced energy consumption. The increasing demand for fuel efficiency and advanced cooling technologies is driving the growth of electric water pumps in modern vehicles.</p></p>
