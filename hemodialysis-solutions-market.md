<p><p>The Hemodialysis Solutions Market consists of two primary types: Hemodialysis Powder (fluid) and Peritoneal Dialysate. Hemodialysis Powder refers to concentrated solutions that are mixed with purified water to create the dialysis fluid used in kidney function replacement treatments. Peritoneal Dialysate is a sterile solution used in peritoneal dialysis, which involves infusing fluid into the abdominal cavity to remove waste products from the bloodstream. Both solutions are essential for managing kidney failure and ensuring effective waste removal from the body.</p></p>
