<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC gas sensor device market includes various types, primarily Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID sensors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, making them highly sensitive to a wide range of volatile organic compounds. In contrast, MOS sensors operate by measuring changes in electrical resistance as gases interact with a heated semiconductor material. Both types serve critical applications in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and air quality management, catering to diverse detection needs and sensitivity requirements.</p></p>
