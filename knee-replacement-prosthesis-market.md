<p><strong>The Knee Replacement Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tibial Component</li><li>Femoral Component</li><li>Patella Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The knee replacement prosthesis market consists of three key components: the tibial component, femoral component, and patella component. The tibial component provides stability and support by replacing the lower leg bone's end, allowing for smooth joint movement. The femoral component replaces the thigh bone's end, facilitating joint articulation. The patella component, or kneecap, ensures proper tracking and movement of the knee joint. Together, these components enhance mobility and reduce pain in patients undergoing knee replacement surgery.</p></p>
