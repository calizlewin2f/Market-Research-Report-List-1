<p><strong>The Orthopaedic Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Navigation Systems</li><li>Electromagnetic Navigation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthopaedic Navigation Systems enhance the precision of surgical procedures. Optical Navigation Systems utilize cameras and infrared technology to track surgical instruments and bones, ensuring accurate positioning during operations. These systems require a clear line of sight and are often favored for their high accuracy. In contrast, Electromagnetic Navigation Systems use electromagnetic fields to determine the locations of instruments and anatomy, allowing for unrestricted movement within the surgical site. This versatility makes them suitable for challenging environments where line-of-sight may be obstructed.</p></p>
