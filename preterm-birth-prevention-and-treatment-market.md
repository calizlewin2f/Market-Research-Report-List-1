<p><p>The preterm birth prevention and treatment market comprises several therapeutic approaches. Progesterone therapy helps reduce the risk of early labor by maintaining pregnancy. Corticosteroid therapy accelerates fetal lung development, vital for preterm infants. Tocolytics therapy delays labor, allowing time for more effective treatments. Antihypertensive therapy manages high blood pressure, which can contribute to preterm birth. Magnesium sulfate therapy is used for neuroprotection in premature infants while also preventing contractions. The "Other" category includes additional treatments tailored to individual patient needs.</p></p>
