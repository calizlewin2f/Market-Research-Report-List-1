<p><strong>The Meat Moisture Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The meat moisture analyzer market primarily features two key technologies: DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared Reflectance) technology. DEXA technology utilizes X-ray methods to measure bone and fat content, offering precise moisture assessment in meat. In contrast, NIR technology employs light reflectance to analyze moisture levels rapidly and non-destructively. Both methods enhance quality control in the meat industry, helping producers maintain optimal product moisture, improve yield, and ensure compliance with labeling and safety standards.</p></p>
