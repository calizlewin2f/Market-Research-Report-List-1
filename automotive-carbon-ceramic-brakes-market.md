<p><p>The automotive carbon ceramic brakes market is divided into two primary types: single disc brake and multiple disc brake systems. Single disc brakes consist of a single rotor used on each wheel, making them simpler and lighter, often found in high-performance vehicles. In contrast, multiple disc brakes feature several rotors per wheel, enhancing braking performance and heat dissipation, suitable for high-end sports cars and racing applications. Both types offer weight reduction, improved stopping power, and resistance to fade, impacting overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
