<p><p>The book sewing machine market is divided into two main types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic machines. Fully-automatic machines are designed for high-volume production, offering enhanced speed and efficiency with minimal manual intervention. They handle various stitching tasks automatically, ensuring consistent quality. In contrast, semi-automatic machines require some manual input during operation, making them suitable for smaller production runs or specific tasks. They provide greater flexibility and are often more cost-effective for businesses with variable output needs.</p></p>
