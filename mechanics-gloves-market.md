<p><p>The mechanics gloves market consists of three primary types: PVC coated, latex coated, and uncoated gloves. Latex coated gloves provide flexibility and high dexterity, ideal for tasks requiring precision, while also offering decent protection against cuts. Uncoated gloves, typically made from durable base materials, provide comfort and breathability, but with less protection than the coated varieties, making them suitable for lighter tasks.</p></p>
