<p><p>The video intercom system market comprises several key types: door stations, video intercom masters, and indoor units. Door stations are installed at entry points, featuring a camera and intercom to facilitate communication and visual identification. Video intercom masters serve as the control hub, allowing users to manage multiple door stations and monitor activities. Indoor units, placed within living or working spaces, enable users to communicate with door stations and view live video feeds, enhancing security and convenience in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
