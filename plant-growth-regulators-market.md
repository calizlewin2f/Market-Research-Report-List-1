<p><ul><li>Auxins</li><li>Cytokinins</li><li>Gibberellins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plant growth regulators market includes various types that influence plant development. Auxins promote cell elongation and root growth, playing a critical role in plant responses to light and gravity. Cytokinins stimulate cell division and promote shoot development, enhancing plant vigor and delaying aging. Gibberellins are responsible for seed germination, flowering, and fruit development, regulating growth and making plants taller and more vigorous. Together, these regulators are essential for optimizing agricultural productivity and improving crop yields.</p></p>
