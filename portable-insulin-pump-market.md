<p><strong>The Portable Insulin Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Pump</li><li>Patch Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable insulin pump market is divided into two main types: normal pumps and patch pumps. Normal pumps, worn on the body, deliver insulin through a tube and require regular cartridge changes. In contrast, patch pumps are tubeless and adhere directly to the skin, offering more flexibility and discretion. Both types provide continuous insulin delivery to manage diabetes more effectively, but they cater to different user preferences in terms of convenience, lifestyle, and comfort.</p></p>
