Light field cameras capture 3D images by recording the intensity and direction of light rays, enabling advanced post-capture refocusing and depth mapping. In contrast, light field displays reproduce these immersive images, providing viewers with a realistic 3D experience without the need for specialized glasses. Together, these technologies enhance visual media, offering new possibilities in photography, virtual reality, and interactive applications.</p></p>
