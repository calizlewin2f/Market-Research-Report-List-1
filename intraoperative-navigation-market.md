<p><p>The intraoperative navigation market is segmented into three main types: optical, electromagnetic, and other systems. Optical navigation utilizes cameras and light systems to track instruments and the surgical site with high precision, making it ideal for minimally invasive procedures. Electromagnetic navigation employs electromagnetic fields to track instruments in real-time, offering flexibility in various surgical environments. Other types may include hybrid systems or those using advanced imaging techniques, catering to specific surgical needs and enhancing operational efficiency. </p></p>
