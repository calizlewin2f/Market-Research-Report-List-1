<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Common Mode Electronic Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-pass</li><li>Low-pass</li><li>Band-pass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Common mode electronic filters are used to suppress unwanted common mode noise in electrical systems. The market is segmented into three main types: high-pass filters, which allow signals above a certain frequency to pass while attenuating lower frequencies; low-pass filters, which do the opposite by allowing lower frequencies to pass and blocking higher ones; and band-pass filters, which permit signals within a specific frequency range to pass while rejecting those outside this range. Each type serves distinct applications in communication and signal processing.</p></p>
